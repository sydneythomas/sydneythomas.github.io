Who is Sydney Allison?

Editor. Developer. Writer. I spent the first five years of my professional life working in the US film and television industry. Although I have worked in various departments over the years, I specialize in post-production and have worked predominately as a VFX editor and senior digital dailies technician.

Following my first work-related international trip, I decided to broaden my horizons and to begin pursuing a new line of work in a new hemisphere. I shifted my focus to software engineering and I am currently working on honing my fullstack skills and bringing them to Europe.

In addition to editing and coding, I have always had an interest in writing. I am currently working on two projects. One is about my travels abroad and the uniquely intersectional experience of being black, female, and American in Europe. The other is a more supportive guide for Americans looking to relocate to Europe.

Recent Experience

Founder, CEO @ Rooted June 2018 - Current

Rooted is a platform for WOC that frequently internationally travel to areas with a large WOC population. Those areas tend to be devoid of products that cater to WOC, and are especially lacking in hair products. Rooted connects travelers with businesses in major cities around the world by adding businesses that carry essential black hair products to an open-source data base.

Fullstack Developer @ Fluido June 2018 - Current

Fluido a language learning platform that connects intermediate-level learners with native speakers via text and video chat. Native speakers can join the platform as teachers in order to encourage learning through having real conversations with real people.

Visit our GitHub

Sr. Digital Dailies Technician @ SIM International September 2014 - Current

As a dailies technician, I have two main repsonsibilities.

The first is to handle the data wrangling and asset management of the media that comes from set. By conducting various back-ups, I secure the original media and catalogue it in the necessary databases. I then update the metadata for video and audio, retime and transcode as necessary, sync the media, and render out deliverables as per specs by production and editorial. I then create and organize projects in editorial's preferred NLE with that day's footage and make sure that the project and proxies are delivered to editorial.

The second responsibility to to act as a liaison between various departments in the workflow pipeline.

For a list of productions that I have worked on, please see the "Projects" section below.

VFX Editor @ Crafty Apes, LLC February 2017 - September 2017

I received the distinct honor of joining Crafty Apes as the company's first VFX Editor. After identifying the needs of the company, I was given free-reign to create a VFX editor workflow that would improve the efficiency of Crafty Apes' three office while preserving their existing workflow pipeline.

I dove head first into the challege of forging a new position and worked to create a standard for the role of VFX editor that helped to improve the quality control and timeliness of turnovers.
